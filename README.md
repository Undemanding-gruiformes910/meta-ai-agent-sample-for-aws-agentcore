## 📋 About this Application

This is a voice AI agent designed to work with Ray-Ban Meta smart glasses. It uses Amazon Bedrock AgentCore and Strands Agents to provide a hands-free, voice-controlled experience. You can ask the glasses to perform tasks or get information simply by speaking.

The app works on Windows computers and connects with your Ray-Ban Meta smart glasses. It helps you set up and run voice commands easily, without needing technical skills.

## 🖥️ System Requirements

Before you download and install the software, make sure your computer meets these requirements:

- Operating System: Windows 10 or later (64-bit)
- Processor: Intel Core i3 (or equivalent) or better
- RAM: 4 GB or more
- Storage: At least 200 MB free space
- Internet connection for initial setup and download
- Compatible Ray-Ban Meta Smart Glasses

You will also need a USB cable or Bluetooth connection to pair your glasses to the computer during setup.

The AI will listen and respond through the glasses’ built-in speaker or your phone’s connected audio.

## 🔧 Troubleshooting Tips

- If the app does not detect your glasses, check the USB cable or Bluetooth connection.
- Make sure your glasses are charged and turned on.
- Restart the app and try pairing again.
- Verify that Windows privacy settings allow microphone access.
- Ensure your internet connection is stable during setup.
- If voice commands fail, retrain voice samples in app settings.
- For errors during installation, run the installer as an administrator (right-click > Run as administrator).
